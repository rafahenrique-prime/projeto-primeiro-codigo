## Agents Module (Configuration Center)

Location: Left sidebar → Agents (3rd icon)

### Basic Info

Core Agent settings:
- **Brand/Company**: Brand name
- **Objective**: Agent's goal
- **Tone**: Communication style
- **Playbook**: Conversation flow / sales script
- **Others**: Additional rules

**Try Me Feature**:
- Located within the Basic Info section
- Allows you to role-play as a customer and interact with the Sales Agent
- Test agent responses in real-time
- Useful for quick testing without going to Chat module

**Web Platform - Using Try Me**:
1. In **Agents** → Select agent → **"Basic Info"** tab
2. Find **"Try Me"** experience module
3. Type messages as if you were a customer
4. Agent responds based on current configuration
5. Use to verify agent behavior after making changes

**APP - Using Try Me**:
1. In **Agents** → Press Test agent button
2. Find **"Try Me"** experience module

### Knowledge

Upload product knowledge to make Agent smarter:
- **Document**: Upload Word/PDF/TXT or URL
- **FAQ**: Add Q&A manually or batch upload via Excel
- **ProductList**: Upload product URLs or screenshots
- **UseCase**: Upload success stories

Tip: Click "View Source" to check original files

### Strategy

Add sales tactics for specific scenarios:
- **Scenario**: Situation description (e.g., "Customer thinks it's expensive")
- **Strategy**: Response approach
- **Script**: Specific talking points

### Chat

Test conversation with Agent:
- Top-right **blue Role Play button**: Test as a customer yourself

### Agent Improvement Tips

**What It Does**: Provide feedback to improve your AI agent's performance after conversations.

**Improvement Areas**:

1. **Tones**:
   - Adjust communication style (formal/casual/friendly/professional)
   - Modify emotional tone and empathy level
   - Fine-tune brand personality expression

2. **Playbook**:
   - Refine conversation scripts and flows
   - Update response templates
   - Improve objection handling techniques

3. **Others**:
   - Custom feedback and guidance
   - Specific scenario refinements
   - Any additional improvements

**How It Works**: After reviewing conversations, provide specific tips. The system uses your feedback to refine agent behavior and responses.

## Inbox Module (Message Center)

### Customer (Real Sales)

- Left side shows real customer list
- Global Autopilot: To the right of the search box at the top of the contact list. 
- Top-right toggle **Autopilot / Copilot** mode:
  - **Copilot**: Agent suggests reply, you confirm and send
  - **Autopilot**: Agent auto-replies to a specific individual user, runs 24/7

### Right Sidebar (During Chat)

- **Objective Progress**: Sales goal progress bar
- **Strategy Analysis**: AI analysis of current situation
- **Recommended Reply**: AI's suggested response

### Message Counting
- **Only Customer module consumes AI Responses**: AI Responses are consumed only when generating recommended replies for your real customers!
- Every AI-generated recommended reply for your real customers counts as 1 message
- Counted whether sent or not (even if you don't send the generated reply)
- Resets monthly

1. Go to **Settings** → **Account** or **Usage** page
2. Click **"Upgrade"** or **"Subscribe"**
3. Choose Monthly or Annual plan
4. Enter payment information
5. Subscription activated immediately

## FAQ
**Q1: How do I connect my WhatsApp/Instagram account to Dealism?**
A1: Go to Channels section. For WhatsApp, scan the QR code. For Instagram, click authorize and log in with your credentials.

**Q2: How does Dealism work for you?**
A2: Dealism is the world's first Vibe Selling AI Sales Agent. It persuades and drives conversions by understanding customer psychology, intent, and emotions. You create a dedicated agent that handles customer interactions on WhatsApp/Instagram with high emotional intelligence, recommends products, and drives sales on your behalf 24/7.

**Q3: What is the value of Dealism?**
A3: The value varies by user. Dealism improves communication efficiency with customers, handles inquiries automatically, and can even complete sales directly without human intervention. It's like having a 24/7 sales representative who never sleeps.

**Q4: What is the subscription price of Dealism?**
A4: Monthly subscription: $39/month (includes all features and 2000 messages). Annual subscription: $384/year (includes all features and 2000 messages per month, saves $84).

**Q5: How are the 2000 monthly messages in Dealism subscription counted?**
A5: In the Inbox's Customer module, every recommended reply generated for a customer counts as 1 message, whether it is sent or not. The count resets monthly.

**Q6: How can I get a free trial of Dealism?**
A6: New accounts registered on dealism.ai receive a 7-day free trial, including all features and 100 messages. No credit card required for trial.

**Q7: How do I upgrade my Dealism subscription plan?**
A7: If you are on the free trial and want to upgrade, go to Settings → Account or Usage page, and directly subscribe to the monthly or annual plan. Payment is processed securely.

**Q8: How does Dealism ensure user data security?**
A8: Dealism currently meets key data certification standards in North and Latin America. All data is encrypted and stored securely. We follow industry best practices for data protection.

**Q9: Why can't some contacts in Dealism Inbox receive messages?**
A9: Mainly due to Instagram restrictions: only contacts who replied to you within the last 24 hours can receive proactive messages. This is an Instagram platform limitation, not a Dealism limitation.

**Q10: Why don't some contacts in Dealism Inbox have profile pictures or nicknames?**
A10: Some channels have their own limitations. For example, Instagram only provides photos and names for the last 20 contacts who replied to you within the last 24 hours. Dealism continues to optimize to provide a better user experience despite these platform limitations.

**Q11: What's the difference between Copilot and Autopilot mode?**
A11: Copilot mode means the agent suggests replies but you must review and confirm before sending. Autopilot mode means the agent automatically sends replies without your confirmation, running 24/7. You can toggle between modes per customer or enable Global Autopilot for all customers at once.

**Q12: Can I edit my agent after creating it?**
A12: Yes! Go to Agents section, select your agent, and edit any component: Basic Info, Knowledge, Strategy, or test in Chat. Changes apply immediately.

**Q13: How many agents can I create?**
A13: Free trial: 1 agent. Paid subscription: Multiple agents (no limit on monthly/annual plans).

**Q14: What file formats are supported for Knowledge upload?**
A14: PDF, Word (DOC/DOCX), Excel (XLS/XLSX), TXT, and website URLs. For FAQ batch upload, use Excel format.

**Q15: How long does it take to train an agent?**
A15: Initial agent creation takes 2-3 minutes for the AI to process your knowledge base. Adding new knowledge or strategies applies in real-time.

## Troubleshooting

**Issue: WhatsApp won't connect**
- Solution: Ensure your phone has internet connection, WhatsApp is updated to latest version, and you're scanning the QR code within the time limit (QR codes expire after ~1 minute).

**Issue: Instagram authorization fails**
- Solution: Check that you're using the correct Instagram account credentials. If previously connected, try disconnecting first then reconnecting. Clear browser cache if issues persist.

**Issue: Agent not responding to customers**
- Solution: Check that Autopilot mode is enabled (toggle in top-right of conversation, or use Global Autopilot). Verify you have remaining messages in your subscription. Check that channels are still connected in Channels section.

**Issue: Agent giving incorrect information**
- Solution: Review and update your Knowledge base. Add more comprehensive information. Create specific Strategies for common scenarios. Use the "Give Tips" feature to provide feedback and improve agent responses.

**Issue: Can't find a specific feature**
- Solution: Use the Quick Reference Guide above to locate features. Check the left navigation bar for main modules. Contact support if feature is still not found.

## Best Practices

1. **Knowledge Base**: Upload comprehensive, accurate information. The more detailed your knowledge base, the better your agent performs.

2. **Strategies**: Create strategies for common customer objections and scenarios. Cover pricing questions, quality concerns, comparison requests, etc.

3. **Testing**: Use the Chat module's Role Play feature to test your agent before going live. Simulate different customer types and scenarios.

4. **Monitoring**: Regularly check Objective Progress in conversations to understand agent performance. Review conversations and provide improvement tips.

5. **Autopilot**: Start with Copilot mode for new agents to review responses. Switch to Autopilot once confident in agent performance.

6. **Updates**: Regularly update your Knowledge base and Strategies as your products, pricing, or policies change.

7. **Feedback Loop**: Use the "Give Tips" feature frequently to continuously improve your agent based on real conversation performance.
